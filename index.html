Infrastructure

Production facility is the spine of any company into manufacturing, as it defines the capacity & capability of the organization and provides confidence and an assurance.

A state – of – art vertically integrated state of the art plant in Bangalore, India is where all the beautiful products are created and produced. Every year over 1.50 Million meters of fabrics flows out of the facility to decorate homes and living spaces across the globe.

Exuding highest respect for the raising concerns on the environment, our plant through its latest technology of effluent treatment makes sure every drop of the effluent is treated, recycled and controlled.

4

In addition to our in-house capacity, we believe in supporting a large community of specialized weavers from handlooms, pit looms and power looms, block printing, manual embroideries, to bring out the best in them. Intelligent mix of our creativity and the non-mechanized skill of these artisans brings out some most beautiful and mind-blowing products and this exactly what inspires the Designers of global brands to work with us.

Factory is well equipped to adept with variety of materials including silk, linen, cotton, wool, polyester, acrylic, nylon, viscose, numerous varieties of blended and fancy yarns, allowing our designers to explore their creativity with no limitations.
